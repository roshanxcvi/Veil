Tools

Veil only blocks client-side trackers — scripts that ship from your browser. It does not block server-side AI crawlers like GPTBot, ClaudeBot, CCBot or Google-Extended, which connect directly to websites without going through you. Site operators have to block those themselves via robots.txt or their CDN.
